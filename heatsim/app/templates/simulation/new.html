Heat Treatment Process

The simulation supports a complete heat treatment cycle:

  1. Heating: Heat up to austenitizing temperature
  2. Transfer: Cool during transfer from furnace to quench
  3. Quenching: Rapid cool in water, oil, or polymer
  4. Tempering: Reheat to tempering temperature (optional)
Geometry Types

Cylinder: Solid round bar or shaft
Plate: Flat plate with symmetric cooling
Ring: Hollow cylinder with inner/outer surfaces
