About Jominy Test

The Jominy end-quench test (ASTM A255) measures steel hardenability by quenching one end of a standard specimen with a water jet.

This creates a gradient of cooling rates:

  • Quenched end: Very fast cooling (~200°C/s)
  • Far end: Slow air cooling (~1°C/s)

J50 is the distance (mm) where hardness drops to 50 HRC - a key hardenability metric.
